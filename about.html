The London Office is one of the UK's most trusted behind-the-scenes B2B service providers, specialising in affordable Registered Office Address, Director Service Address, Business Address, Company Formations and Telephone Answering services.

Since starting with a single London office in 2014, we have now grown to seven prestigious city centre offices in two countries, with even more locations in the pipeline.

Our client base is diverse, with companies from all over the world using the security of a UK company address to trade both nationally and internationally.

    The business continued to grow, and we strengthened our position by nearly doubling our number of employees.

  • 2020

    In October, our fourth Central London location will open at 167-169 Great Portland Street, offering a new meeting room space.

  • 2021

    We will be looking at new office locations in Edinburgh, Manchester, Cardiff, Cambridge and Gibraltar.
