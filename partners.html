Frequently Asked Questions

Discover some of our most asked questions about our Partner Program

How can I become a Partner?
Complete the enquiry form on this page and we will get back to you regarding setting up a Partner account.
Is a minimum number of companies required?
Our Partner packages start from 11 companies upwards.
Do I receive a discount as a Partner?
Yes. The more clients you have, the greater your discount on our packages. Our Bronze, Silver, Gold and Platinum packages all offer fantastic discounts.
Will you forward mail to my individual clients?
No, all mail is forwarded or scanned and emailed to you. It is up to you to distribute it to your individual clients.
Do you need ID for my individual clients?
No, we only require your ID as the account holder. It is your responsibility to do your own checks on your individual clients.
